My proposal:

Employee remuneration and expenses (earning over $75,000) 

	Who are the expected users?
		Vancouver residents interested in government salary transparency
		City analysts or journalists researching salary data	
		Anyone curious about high-earning City of Vancouver employees

	What problem is the application meant to solve?
		Making it easy for users to browse, filter, and provide Feedback/Rating on city employees earning over $75,000/year, supporting transparency and public engagement
		
	What are some operations on the data that will need to be implemented?
		Filter: Employes Employees by Year, Department, and Earnings
		View: Job title and the Salary, and Name of employess
		Feedback: For Each employee user can provide feed back regarding the salary like(High, low, acceptable) which will be stored in database.	
		
	What data is necessary for your application? What data must you store?
		Name, Job title, Department, Remuneration, Expenses, Reporting year and Rating
		Data set:https://opendata.vancouver.ca/explore/embed/dataset/employee-remuneration-and-expenses-earning-over-75000/table/?disjunctive.department&disjunctive.title&sort=year
